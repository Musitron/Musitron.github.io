The Core Problem

It is not a lack of discipline. It is a lack of direction.

Most guitarists do not fall short due to a lack of practice hours — they fall short because they practice without a strategic blueprint, repeating the same mechanical errors right up until the day of the exam or competition.

We work together on what truly shifts the needle: technique directly applied to the exact repertoire you are scheduled to perform, utilizing targeted feedback so that every single hour in the studio yields maximum results.

About Me

Manuel Olivares

Classical guitarist holding a Master of Music degree from Appalachian State University (USA). With over 30 years dedicated to the instrument, my pedagogical approach draws not only from the concert stage but also from the science of human movement.

For several years, I operated as a researcher within an international team developing advanced methodologies for bodily optimization and motor efficiency applied to musical performance. Today, working independently, I integrate that specialized background directly into my private teaching practice.

The objective is straightforward: to equip you with the precise tools required to master the core repertoire (from Bach and Tárrega to Barrios and Llobet), optimize your ergonomics, and achieve peak artistic output while minimizing physical fatigue.
